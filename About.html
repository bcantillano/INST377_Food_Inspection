About

The purpose of our website:

  • There is no way for people to see what restaurants, food inspection,and areas are compliant and not compliant in the prince george county area. On our website, residents of the prince George county area can find restaurants whether pass food inspections or not.

Our function:

  • We create a coordinate based on a map of Prince George County to show the inspection status of all restaurants to the user.

Stakeholders:

  • Prince George's County Residents
  • Students
  • Restaurant manager

Technology Stack

  • Node.js code
  • HTML/CSS
  • Leaflet JavaScript API
